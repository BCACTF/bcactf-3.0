Make Brownies

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup butter, melted
  2. 2 cups white sugar
  3. ½ cup cocoa powder
  4. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  5. 4 eggs
  6. 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  7. ½ teaspoon baking powder
  8. ½ teaspoon salt
  9. ½ cup walnut halves

Procedure

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch pan.
  2. Combine the melted butter, sugar, cocoa powder, vanilla, eggs, flour, baking powder, and salt. Spread the batter into the prepared pan. Add the walnut halves for decoration.
  3. Bake in preheated oven for 20 to 30 minutes
  4. Cool on wire rack.
