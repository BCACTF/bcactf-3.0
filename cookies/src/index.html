Make Cookies

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup (2 sticks) butter or margarine, softened.
  2. ½ cup firmly packed light brown sugar
  3. 1 large egg
  4. 1 tsp vanilla extract
  5. 2 cup all-purpose flour
  6. ¾ tsp baking soda
  7. ¾ tsp salt
  8. 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  9. ⅔ cup granulated sugar

Procedure

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Cream together the margarine, sugar and brown sugar until light and fluffy, scrape down often.
  3. Beat in vanilla and egg until emulsified.
  4. Combine the flour, baking soda, and salt and sift in a separate bowl.
  5. Blend in the dry ingredients after the margarine, sugars, vanilla and eggs are creamed well.
  6. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  7. Store, cover tightly and refrigerate dough if not using immediately.
  8. Scoop onto ungreased parchment paper.
  9. Bake for 8-10 min. or until lightly browned.*
  10. Cool for 10 minutes.
