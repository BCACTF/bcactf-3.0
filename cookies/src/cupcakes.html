Make Cupcakes

Ingredients

  1. 2 large eggs (room temperature)
  2. 1 cup granulated sugar
  3. 1 stick butter (½ cup), room temperature
  4. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  5. 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  6. 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  7. 1 pinch of salt
  8. ½ cup milk

Procedure

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Line your pan with cupcake liners.
  2. Beat together eggs and granulated sugar. Then, add softened butter and the vanilla extract. Mix until creamy.
  3. Sift together the flour, baking powder, and salt in a small bowl.
  4. Alternating with milk, add the dry ingredients to the egg-and-sugar mixture with three increments. Mix well, but do not mix after the solution is combined.
  5. Scoop cupcake batter into the lined muffin pan. Be careful not to overfill them, as they will rise as they bake. Bake for 17-20 minutes
  6. Remove your cupcakes from the oven and let sit for about 2 minutes.
